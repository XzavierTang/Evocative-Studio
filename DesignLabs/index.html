Information design is the practice of
presenting information in a way that fosters an
efficient and effective understanding of the information.
The term has come to be used for a specific area of graphic design
related to displaying information effectively,
rather than just attractively or for artistic expression.
Information design is closely related to the field of data visualization
and is often taught as part of graphic design courses.
According to Per Mollerup,information design is explanation design.
It explains facts of the universe and leads to knowledge and informed action.
